Lib-STATIC is a minimal computing-based approach to creating data-driven tools that leverages static-web technologies and metadata skills to create engaging web publications and applications.

Are you:

  • Tired of learning systems rather than skills?
  • Frustrated with overbloated infrastcture?
  • Siloed by the tools you know and the tools you don't?

Try the Lib-STATIC approach and let's all rethink how we do web development and systems in the libraries.

(It might work for you, or you might hate it. But at least you tried.)
